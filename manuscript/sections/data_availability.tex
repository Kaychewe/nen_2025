\subsection{\texorpdfstring{Data Availability
}{Data Availability }}\label{data-availability}

Formalin-fixed, paraffin-embedded (FFPE) tumor tissues were collected
under IRB-approved protocols at Fox Chase Cancer Center. Additional
access may be available upon reasonable request, subject to
institutional review and material transfer agreement. Gene expression
data generated in this study are provided in Supplementary Data 1.
Public datasets used include GEO accessions GSE118014, GSE211483, and
GSE211486.\cite{GSE118014,GSE211483,GSE211486}
Analysis scripts, trained models, and visualization tools are available
in the project repository \cite{KaycheweNEN2025}.
